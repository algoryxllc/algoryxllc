Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Algoryx provide? +
Algoryx provides IT and technology solutions including cloud services, network infrastructure, cybersecurity, managed IT support, and curated tech product sales for businesses and consumers.
How do I contact Algoryx support? +
You can contact support via our Contact page or by email at support@algoryxllc.com. For immediate inquiries use the Get a Quote button.
What are your office hours? +
Live chat and phone support are available Monday – Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM (MST). Outside these hours, submit a form and we will respond on the next business day.
How long does shipping take? +
Orders are processed within 1–3 business days. Typical delivery: US: 5–8 business days; International: 10–15 business days (customs may add time).
Do you offer returns or refunds? +
Yes. Returns and refunds are handled per our Returns & Refund Policy. Customers must initiate requests within the specified time window and provide proof of purchase.

Yes. We work with resellers, distributors, and business partners. Use the Contact page to share your company details and partnership proposal.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept major credit/debit cards, PayPal, Wise, and approved corporate billing arrangements where applicable.
How can I track my order? +
After shipment you will receive an email with a tracking number and carrier link. Use that information to monitor delivery status on the carrier's website.
